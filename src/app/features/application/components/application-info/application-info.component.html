Who We Are

UKSF is a British Special Forces milsim unit that's been running for over 10 years. We take the game seriously without taking ourselves seriously. No forced "yes sir" nonsense, no parade ground roleplay. We get the job done and we do it well, and we have a laugh while doing it. When things go wrong in-game, we switch on and perform.

A good number of our members and leadership are active or ex-service. The experience is real, but this is a game and we treat it like one. Our leadership has been in the unit for 10+ years, and several members even longer.


What We Do

Main operations are every Saturday evening at 7:45pm — this is the centrepiece of the week

Training is every Wednesday at 7:45pm

Side operations run Tuesday and Thursday from 7:30pm, and occasionally other days. These aren't mandatory but they're good fun

We debrief after every operation. Performance gets analysed and discussed — it's how we stay sharp


Who Fits Here

We're looking for people who stick around. This isn't a revolving door — our core members have been here for years. We want mature players who are proactive and engaged, not people who'll disappear after a month

You should be able to commit to most Saturdays. If you can't make it, just submit a LOA through the website — life comes first, but we need to know

No dual-clanning. If you're actively part of another milsim unit, this isn't the right fit


Your Path In

After your application is accepted, you'll start Basic Training — 4 phases over a minimum of 4 weeks, depending on your attendance and instructor availability

Once you pass Basic, you move into SFSG where the real depth opens up. Section qualifications (gunner, marksman, radio, recce), role specialisations (NCO, Officer, Medic), and unit specialisations (Sniper, JSFAW) are all available

Specialisations are earned through demonstrated ability and attitude, not just time served


Requirements

You must own a legitimate copy of Arma 3 with the APEX DLC

Your system needs to handle modded Arma at reasonable performance — our missions can be intensive
