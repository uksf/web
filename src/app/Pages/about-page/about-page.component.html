UKSF

We are a like-minded community dedicated to fun and immersion


The unit is based on a real task force responsible for British special forces missions around the globe


Our members carry out a multitude of mission types in a variety of environments using a broad spectrum of skills


Whilst we teach our members the basics of Arma soldier skills, we are not for the casual Arma player

Our values

Regardless of age, members are expected to conduct themselves in a mature fashion


Everyone is ultimately here to have fun. Operations are tailored to challenge both the team and the individual, whilst remaining engaging and enjoyable for all


UKSF operates in units, which are further split into small teams. We expect members to be able to function in a team without issue


Like the units we try to simulate, we expect a continuously good skill level from members. Everyone must hold themselves and their peers to a high standard


We aim to achieve as high a level of immersion as possible on our operations, covering everything from communications and procedures, to analysis of intel and operational documentation

Special Forces

The Special Forces playstyle is the core of UKSF operations. The various teams within UKSF take on a variety of roles and specialisations including but not limited to: Airborne, Waterborne, and Mobility


Variety is a key part of playing as Special Forces and as such, our missions and campaigns can cover everything from large scale operations, stealth strikes against key targets, hiding in plain sight in civilian clothing, counter-insurgency operations, insurgent operations, and everything in between


Our members attain the necessary skills through training and specialisation courses, with ample opportunity for practice. This ensures that everyone knows their role, and standards on operations are kept high to maximise peoples' enjoyment

Aviation

Our high-standards dedicated aviation wing provides ground units with vital support in the field, and plays an important role in the success of each operation


We offer roles in rotary transport and attack aircraft, and various fixed-wing aircraft


Each role is responsible for a variety of missions in support of ground and other air units, and pilots are held to the highest standard during every mission they undertake


In addition to our in-depth procedures, full Air Traffic Control and Combat Air Controller communications creates a rich simulation environment

Training

Frequent training enables us to achieve a high level of realism and immersion


A 4 week basic training course is completed by all members before specialising in any role


Specialist training courses enable members to immerse themselves in rewarding roles and contribute to the mission in a unique way


The specialist roles we offer include Medic, Sniper, FAC, NCO, and Officer. In addition to these roles, there are qualifications that enable the use of specialist weapon systems, such as anti-tank, gunner, and marksman

When we play

Our main operations are every Saturday at 7:45pm BST. We expect all members to attend this event on a regular basis


Training sessions run every Wednesday at 7:45pm BST. Specialist units also train on an ad-hoc basis on other days


Side operations run most days of the week at 7:30pm BST. These are optional but encouraged

Getting started

If you are interested in becoming a member of UKSF, fill in an application


A recruitment officer will contact you to organise a trial session once you submit an application


If you have questions before you apply, please speak to any of our members on TeamSpeak


Or you can talk with us on our Discord
